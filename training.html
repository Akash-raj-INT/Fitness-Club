Training: Body Part Exercises

Chest Exercises

Bench Press

A classic compound movement that targets the pectoral muscles, shoulders, and triceps. Can be performed with a barbell or dumbbells.

Push-Ups

Bodyweight exercise that works the chest, shoulders, and triceps. Variations include incline, decline, and diamond push-ups.

Chest Fly

Isolation exercise performed with dumbbells or cables to stretch and contract the chest muscles.

Back Exercises

Pull-Ups

Bodyweight exercise that targets the upper back, lats, and biceps. Can be performed with various grips.

Barbell Row

Compound movement that works the middle and upper back, as well as the biceps and forearms.

Lat Pulldown

Machine exercise that isolates the latissimus dorsi muscles, helping to build a wider back.

Shoulder Exercises

Overhead Press

Targets the deltoid muscles and triceps. Can be performed standing or seated, with a barbell or dumbbells.

Lateral Raise

Isolation exercise for the side deltoids, performed with dumbbells or cables.

Front Raise

Targets the front deltoids, helping to build shoulder size and strength.

Arm Exercises

Bicep Curl

Isolation exercise for the biceps, performed with dumbbells, barbells, or cables.

Tricep Dips

Bodyweight exercise that targets the triceps. Can be performed on parallel bars or a bench.

Hammer Curl

Variation of the bicep curl that also works the forearms.

Leg Exercises

Squats

Compound movement that targets the quadriceps, hamstrings, glutes, and lower back.

Lunges

Works the quads, glutes, and hamstrings. Can be performed with bodyweight or added resistance.

Leg Press

Machine exercise that isolates the quadriceps, glutes, and hamstrings.

Core Exercises

Plank

Isometric exercise that strengthens the entire core, including abs, obliques, and lower back.

Crunches

Targets the rectus abdominis ("six-pack" muscles). Can be performed on the floor or with equipment.

Russian Twist

Works the obliques and helps improve rotational strength and stability.
